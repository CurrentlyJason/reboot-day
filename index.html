The Concept

Inspired by Bill Gates' famous "Think Week" retreats

Taken on one of my favorite Reboot Day trips

Reboot Day is a monthly practice designed to help you pause, reflect, and realign with your goals and values. In our constantly connected world, we rarely take time to step back and assess our direction.

Just as Bill Gates takes his famous "Think Week" retreats to disconnect and focus on big ideas, Reboot Day gives you a structured framework to do the same on a monthly basis.

How Reboot Day Works

A simple four-step process to transform your month

1

Choose Your Day

Pick one day each month to dedicate entirely to yourself. Mark it in your calendar as non-negotiable. Choose a favorite spot or explore somewhere new—just make sure it's distraction-free and yours for the day.

2

Set Your Focus

Define what you want from the month ahead. Clarify your goals, expectations, and the mindset you want to carry forward.

3

Disconnect & Reflect

Step away from routine. Go for a hike, find a quiet café, or walk in nature. A change of environment sparks fresh thinking. Turn off notifications, follow the guided structure, and reflect on the last month—celebrating wins and learning from what didn't work.

4

Plan With Intention

Look forward with clarity. Set meaningful goals for next month, align them with your values, and create a plan that supports the life you actually want to live.
